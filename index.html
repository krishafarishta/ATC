About Us

ATC has been a trusted name in manufacturing and supply since 2000, proudly rooted in Bengaluru.

We specialise in the manufacture of steel wool and steel fibre, alongside a curated selection of scrubbers and industrial supplies. Our products serve a wide range of industries — from construction to cleaning — built to perform wherever they're needed.

Over two decades in business, we've grown through the trust of loyal customers and the strength of lasting relationships. We don't just supply products — we understand what our customers need and go the extra mile to deliver it, even sourcing and trading in additional products as opportunities arise.

When you work with ATC, you're working with experience you can count on.
